Our Mission

More Than Media — A Movement

HWARRA (Health, Wellness, ARRA) exists at the intersection of broadcast media and community wellness. We believe that when people are informed, inspired, and healthy, communities thrive. Our 9-channel network and high-tech wellness platform are built to make that vision reality — for everyone.
